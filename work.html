Field and Body of Work

Mary Agnes Chase was a prolific botanist. She began her work doing scientific illustrations but quickly shifted to doing important field research. Chase's main field of research was on grasses. Her work was some of the first compiled research on grass. She researched grass in the continental U.S. and in Brazil. Chase's work in Brazil expanded the world's knowledge of Brazilian grasses by at least 10 percent!
