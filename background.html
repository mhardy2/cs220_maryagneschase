Mary Agnes Chase was born in Illinois to an industrious mother and an Irish, blacksmith father. Her father died when she was just two years old. As a young woman working at the local newspaper, Chase married William Ingraham Chase the editor of the paper. Tragically William died one year after marrying Mary. After her tragic loss Mary moved in with her brother in law and began on her journey as a botanist.
